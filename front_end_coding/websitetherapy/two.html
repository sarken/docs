alts

Say hi to the [alt] attribute. An attribute is information you attach to a tag, like title, class, or src. <a href="http://www...">You know href already</a>.

Alts describe your images. Not everyone loads images, so it's important to do this. A splash page with a picture for the link and no alt is a completely blank page to some users.

You should only describe pictures that have an alternative meaning. Don't write alt="spacer" or alt="background". That would be annoying, wouldn't it?

thumbnails

Pictures that take a long time to download are a barrier. Make thumbnails - smaller preview pictures - and link to your full size images. Cut down the size of your banner.

A good size is 200px high.
